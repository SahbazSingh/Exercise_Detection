About Us

Welcome to Jatt Fitness AI

Jatt Fitness AI is dedicated to helping you achieve your fitness goals through the power of artificial intelligence. Our gymAIapp allows users to upload videos of their workout sessions and receive real-time feedback on their exercise form and technique, focusing on exercises like bicep curls, hammer curls, and more.

Our mission is to make fitness more accessible and effective by providing detailed, AI-driven insights into your workouts. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced athlete, our platform is designed to help you optimize your performance and ensure you're exercising safely and effectively.

Our Technology

At the heart of our application is a cutting-edge machine learning model trained to detect and analyze exercise movements. This model processes video data to identify key aspects of exercise form, providing users with feedback that can help prevent injuries and improve exercise outcomes.
